## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Projects dashboard">
    Click on "Projects" in the main menu after logging in.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click ‘Add New Project’">
    Find and click the “Add New Project” button at the top right.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill the details">
    Enter your project name and optional description.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create Project">
    Click "Create" to finish. Your new project appears in the list.
  </Step>
</Steps>
