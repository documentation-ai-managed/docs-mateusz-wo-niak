---
title: Document Changes Not Syncing
description: Fix when documentation updates aren't visible.
---

## Symptoms
- Changes to docs not showing
- Edits appear missing for other users

## Quick Fix
<Steps>
  <Step title="Refresh your browser">
    Try a hard refresh (`Ctrl+F5` or `Cmd+Shift+R`).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Re-sync documentation">
    Click the "Sync Now" button in the dashboard.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="tip">Still not working? Log out and log back in or contact support.</Callout>
